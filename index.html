ABOUT

This isn't another keynote conference.

Tokio Desing Forum is a small, independent gathering--50 designers, founders, and buiders who are actually shipping. thee value isn't just the talks. it's being in the room with each other.

Two days in Tokyo. Real conversations. Connections that last.

WHY TOKYO

The future is built together.

For too long, the best builders only gathered in one city.Meanwhile, Tokyo perfected craft at scale--invisible systems, uncompromising quality

Western innovation meets Eastern precision. One conversation, now two separate ones.

FAQ

Questions?

Who is Tokyo Design Forum for?

+

Designers, founders, and builders actually shipping at the edge of design, product, and AI. The people who see design as leverage, not decoration . Whether you're independent or leading a team, you'll be surrounded by others who care about craft, culture, and conversation—not hype.

Why Tokyo? What makes this different from every other design conference?

+

Because Tokyo is design—systems that anticipate instead of interrupt, craftsmanship that never compromises. Unlike massive corporate conferences, TDF is small, intentional, and focused on the real conversations that happen between talks. We're here to bridge the creative energy of East and West—designing the future together, not separately.

What's included with my ticket?

+

Two days of sessions, panels, and evening gatherings at Trunk Hotel in Shibuya, plus lifetime access to the TDF community. Lunch is on your own (Tokyo's food scene is half the experience), but we'll organize group reservations and hangouts nearby.

How many people will be there?

+

Only 50 attendees. That's intentional. You'll actually meet everyone—no velvet ropes, no speaker green rooms, no filler panels. Just creative people in one room, learning from each other.

I want to sponsor. How does that work?

+

We're open to a handful of design-aligned partners to help make year one. Sponsorship isn't about logo walls—it's about genuine alignment. We'll work with you to create something meaningful: from experiences to content integrations.

What's the refund policy?

+

If the event doesn't proceed, tickets are fully refundable. Otherwise, tickets are non-refundable but transferable up to January 31, 2026.

How do I justify this to my company or team?

+

TDF isn't a typical conference—it's a convergence of design, product, and culture. Attendees leave with global connections, cross-disciplinary insights, and inspiration that translates directly to better teams, better products, and better thinking. It's the kind of professional development that can't be streamed or summarized.
